About the Doctor

Meet Dr. Gargi Singh Thakur

10+ Years Experience

Dedicated to Your Health & Well-being

Dr. Gargi Singh Thakur is an experienced physician specialising in internal medicine with over 10 years of professional expertise. She earned her MBBS from Vydehi University Institute of Medical Sciences and completed her MD in Internal Medicine from Rajiv Gandhi University of Health Sciences, Karnataka (2017).

She has served as a senior resident for 3 years in reputed medical institutions in Bhubaneswar. Her clinical focus spans metabolic diseases, infectious diseases, respiratory conditions, rheumatological care, and critical care management.

Education MBBS, MD (Internal Medicine) - RGUHS, Karnataka
Membership Member, European Congress of Rheumatology
Languages Odia, Bengali, Hindi, Kannada, English
Publications Research in H. Pylori, Pyrexia & Tubercular Liver Abscess
